WHAT IS THIS?

We wanted to put together a workshop series for product, ux and brand designers to level up their game one bite size skill at a time.

There are no slidedecks, no presentations, no books to be sold, no presenters and no pretentious clickbait echo chamber rants…

Just small, one hour, hands on, real skill workshops for real people… from real people.
